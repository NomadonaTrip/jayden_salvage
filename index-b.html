What We
Buy & Sell

We'll Take the Car

Running or not, we buy vehicles in any condition. Drive it in or tell us where it sits — we assess it, make you an offer, and pay you the same day. All paperwork handled on-site.

Scrap Metal Buying

Copper, aluminum, steel, brass — bring it in and we weigh it on-site. Our rates track the commodity market daily, so you always get a fair number. Cash in hand before you leave.
